## Objection Handlers

**"How are you different from SleekFlow?"**
> "SleekFlow gives you tools to build automation yourself. We give you an AI team that executes — plus humans who handle the complex stuff. You don't configure anything. It just works."

**"Why should I pay when I can use spreadsheets?"**
> "Spreadsheets aren't a system. They don't execute. They don't follow up. They don't remind you. AI Staff does all that automatically. No system, no growth."

**"That seems expensive for an SME."**
> "At Rp5.5 million per month, you're paying less than one entry-level employee — but getting an AI team that works 24/7. And when things get complex, real humans help. It's the system that lets you scale."

**"What about AI replacing humans?"**
> "AI handles the repetitive stuff — follow-ups, reminders, logging. Humans handle what AI can't — strategy, complex decisions, relationship building. Together, they're more effective than either alone."

**"How is this different from a chatbot?"**
> "Chatbots respond to customers. Our AI Staff executes your operations — they chase leads, send reminders, generate reports, track expenses. They're your back-office team, not a customer-facing bot."
