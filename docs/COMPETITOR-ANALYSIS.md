## Executive Summary

SleekFlow is an omnichannel messaging automation platform targeting commerce-driven businesses. They focus on **self-service automation tools** while my21staff offers **AI team that executes + humans for complex decisions**.

| Aspect | SleekFlow | my21staff |

| Model | DIY automation platform | AI executes routine + humans handle complex |
| Target | Global SME to Enterprise | Indonesian UMKM |
| Strength | Feature-rich, integrations | AI team that actually executes |
| Weakness | Complex setup, self-service only | Fewer channels |

## Meta Ads Integration (Click-to-WhatsApp)

### SleekFlow's CTWA Solution

SleekFlow has deep Meta Ads integration:

1. **Flow Builder Trigger for CTWA**
   - Automatically start conversation flow when user clicks WhatsApp ad
   - Qualify leads with automated questions
   - Route to human agent based on responses

2. **Auto-Save Ad Source**
   - Tracks which ad/campaign generated the lead
   - Attribution data stored in CRM
   - ROI tracking per campaign

3. **First-Party Data Capture**
   - Collects customer data through chat (bypasses iOS privacy restrictions)
   - Builds retargeting audiences
   - No dependency on third-party cookies

4. **Conversion Tracking**
   - Track lead → customer journey
   - Measure cost per qualified lead
   - A/B test ad → chat flows

### my21staff Gap

We mention Meta Ads integration as a "future upsell" but have no current implementation:

- ❌ No CTWA automation trigger
- ❌ No ad source tracking
- ❌ No conversion attribution
- ❌ No first-party data capture flow

**Priority:** HIGH - This is critical for lead gen businesses.

## Strengths & Weaknesses

### SleekFlow Strengths

1. **Omnichannel** - 9+ channels in one inbox
2. **Flow Builder** - Visual automation, no code
3. **Meta Ads integration** - CTWA automation is killer feature
4. **eCommerce ready** - Shopify, payments, catalog
5. **Free tier** - Low barrier to entry
6. **Enterprise features** - RBAC, SSO, compliance

### SleekFlow Weaknesses

1. **Self-service only** - User must configure everything
2. **No human support** - Email-only, limited chat
3. **Complex for SMEs** - Too many features, steep learning curve
4. **Price increases** - Recently raised prices significantly
5. **Global focus** - Not localized for Indonesian market

### my21staff Strengths

1. **AI executes for you** - AI Staff do the routine work, not just respond
2. **Humans for complex decisions** - Real people when AI can't solve it
3. **AI Staff model** - 8 specialists for each business function
4. **Indonesian market** - Local language, local pricing, UMKM focus
5. **Website included** - Lead gen pages built-in
6. **Simple pricing** - No per-contact scaling

### my21staff Weaknesses

1. **Fewer channels** - Focused on key messaging platforms
2. **No automation builder** - Can't create custom flows
3. **No free tier** - Higher barrier to entry
4. **No eCommerce** - No Shopify, no payments
5. **Limited integrations** - Fewer third-party connections
6. **Limited analytics** - No dashboards (yet)

## Meta WhatsApp API Billing

### Pricing Model (Effective July 2025)

Meta changed from **conversation-based** to **per-message pricing**:

| Message Type | Description | Cost Range (USD) |
|--------------|-------------|------------------|
| **Marketing** | Promotions, offers, announcements | $0.02 - $0.08 per message |
| **Utility** | Order updates, shipping, receipts | $0.01 - $0.03 per message |
| **Authentication** | OTP, verification codes | $0.01 - $0.02 per message |
| **Service** | Customer-initiated within 24hr window | **FREE** |

*Costs vary by recipient country. Indonesia rates are on the lower end.*

### Free Messaging

| Scenario | Free? |
|----------|-------|
| Customer messages you first (24hr window) | ✅ Free |
| You reply within 24 hours | ✅ Free |
| Click-to-WhatsApp ad entry (72hr window) | ✅ Free |
| Utility templates within service window | ✅ Free |
| Marketing template outside 24hr window | ❌ Paid |
| First 1,000 service conversations/month | ✅ Free |

### Cost Example: Indonesian Business

| Scenario | Monthly Volume | Est. Meta Cost |
|----------|----------------|----------------|
| Small business | 500 marketing msgs | ~$15-20 |
| Medium business | 2,000 marketing msgs | ~$50-80 |
| Large business | 10,000 marketing msgs | ~$250-400 |

**Note:** Service conversations (customer-initiated) are free. Optimize for inbound!

### SleekFlow's Meta Billing

- SleekFlow is a **WhatsApp BSP** (Business Solution Provider)
- They mark up Meta's rates (typically 10-20%)
- First 1,000 service conversations free per month
- Broadcast/template costs passed through + markup

### my21staff Billing Model

- We use **Kapso** as our WhatsApp provider
- Meta costs pass through to client (or bundled in tier)
- Need to clarify: Are we absorbing Meta costs or passing through?

**Decision needed:** Should we bundle WhatsApp message costs in subscription or pass through?
