**Last Updated:** 2026-01-15

## How It Works

Existing customers earn recurring commissions for every new customer they refer.

| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| **First month** | Rp500,000 |
| **Ongoing** | Rp200,000/month |
| **Duration** | No cap — earn as long as customer stays |

## Terms & Conditions

1. **Eligibility**: You must be an active paying customer to earn referral commissions
2. **Self-referrals**: Referring yourself or your own business accounts is not allowed
3. **Abuse**: Fake or fraudulent referrals will result in account termination
4. **Payout stop**: If the referred customer cancels, your commission stops immediately
5. **Changes**: We reserve the right to modify the program with 30 days notice

## FAQ

**Q: Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?**
A: No limit. Refer as many as you want.

**Q: What if my referral upgrades from Solo to Team?**
A: Your commission rate stays the same (Rp200k/month). No change.

**Q: What if my referral downgrades or pauses?**
A: If they stay subscribed (even at a lower tier), you keep earning. If they cancel, payout stops.

**Q: Can I refer someone who already contacted my21staff?**
A: Only new customers count. If they already had a conversation with us, it doesn't count as a referral.

**Q: How do I track my referrals?**
A: Dashboard tracking coming soon. For now, contact support.
