## Target Market (3 Niches)

### 1. Side-Hustle Professionals
Corporate managers, engineers, doctors earning $100K+ day jobs. Can't reply during work hours. Want an "automated cash machine" to eventually exit 9-5.

**Ideal for:** E-learning, consulting, high-ticket services

### 2. Overwhelmed Solopreneurs
Making Rp 15M-50M/month, running a 1-person sales team. Spending 4+ hours/day on "Berapa harganya?" — and losing money every minute not spent on real sales.

**Need:** An "Admin" without the human overhead

### 3. Niche High-End Service Providers
Clinics, property agents, Hajj/Umrah advisors. One lead is worth millions. Missing a chat = massive lost revenue.

**Ideal for:** Aesthetic/Dental clinics, Real Estate, Premium Travel
