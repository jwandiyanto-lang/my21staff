# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Game Plan: 3 Phases to 50 Clients

---

## Phase 1: The "Genesis" (Months 1–3)
**Goal:** 5 Founding Clients | **Focus:** Perfect the system

### Month 1: The Beta Launch
- Get your first 2 clients from immediate network or Side-Hustle professionals
- Focus: Perfecting the Kapso bridge and the Intern/Brain "Handover" logic
- Price: "Founding Member" discount (Rp 7.5M setup instead of Rp 12M)

### Month 2: The Nurture Build
- Sign 2 more clients
- Build first iteration of the "Brain Bot" automated newsletter
- Start documenting case studies

### Month 3: The Case Study
- Sign 1 client (total: 5)
- Document "Before vs. After" of your first Month 1 client
- This story becomes your primary sales tool

**Phase 1 Revenue Target:** ~Rp 70M

---

## Phase 2: The "Niche Authority" (Months 4–6)
**Goal:** Reach 15 Clients | **Focus:** One niche, proven play

### Month 4: The Price Hike
- Stop "Founding Member" discounts
- Move to $750 (Rp 12M) setup fee
- Focus on ONE specific niche (e.g., Aesthetic Clinics in Jakarta)

### Month 5: The Omnichannel Push
- Implement Instagram/Facebook "Plan B" bridge for top-tier clients
- Use results (increased DM to WhatsApp conversion) to create faceless content
- Begin Meta Ads at Rp 5M/month

### Month 6: The "2-a-Month" Rhythm
- Steady flow of referrals
- Goal: 15 active subscribers
- **Cashflow Check:** Setup fees = ~$11,000 (Rp 175M+)

**Phase 2 Revenue Target:** ~Rp 250M

---

## Phase 3: The "Scale" (Months 7–12)
**Goal:** 50 Clients & Rp 185M MRR | **Focus:** Content machine + hiring

### Months 7-9: The Content Machine
- Use your own "Brain Bot" to generate marketing content
- Focus on "Professional Side-Hustlers" on LinkedIn and Instagram
- Scale Meta Ads to Rp 10M/month

### Months 10-12: The 50-Client Push
- Need 5-8 clients/month during this quarter
- Hire part-time VA for basic Kapso connections
- Focus on "Studio" level deals

**Phase 3 Revenue Target:** ~Rp 600M+
