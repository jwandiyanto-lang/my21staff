**Strategy based on:** Samuel Thompson's "Divorce Bible" method for high-converting offers

---

## Core Philosophy

Samuel didn't just sell a "book"; he sold "The Divorce Bible: How to Win Your Divorce." It's specific, outcome-focused, and targets a painful emotional state. You're not selling a "WhatsApp CRM."

## Step 1: Nail Your "Divorce Bible" Angle & Offer

### Find Your Angle: Core, Painful Problem

**Bad:**
- "A CRM that integrates with WhatsApp."

**Good:**
- "Stop Losing Sales to WhatsApp Chaos."
- "The Only CRM That Turns Your WhatsApp Mess into a Money-Making Machine."
- "Finally, a CRM Your Team Will Actually Use for WhatsApp."

### Structure Your Offer (The "Starter Kit")

Samuel increased AOV by bundling. Offer a **"WhatsApp Business Growth Kit"** instead of just a free trial.

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Core Offer** | 14-Day Free Trial of 21staff.com |
| **Bonus Gift #1** | "The Ultimate WhatsApp Sales Template Pack" (PDF: 25 copy-paste templates for closing, objections, follow-ups) |
| **Bonus Gift #2** | "Customer Support Response Cheat Sheet" (One-page guide for common support queries) |
| **Bonus Gift #3** | Access to exclusive "WhatsApp Power Users" community (Discord/Facebook group) |

**Why this works:** Conversion rate increases because value proposition is no longer "try my software" â€” it's "get everything you need to master WhatsApp for your business."

## Step 2: Build a High-Converting Landing Page

Samuel runs 90% of ads to a product page, not a homepage. You need a dedicated landing page designed for one thing: **getting a sign-up**.

### Platform
- Leadpages, Unbounce, or highly optimized Shopify/WordPress page
- Conversion-focused design (not information-focused)

### Copywriting with ChatGPT

**Prompt for Headline:**
> "Generate 10 high-converting headlines for a landing page selling a WhatsApp CRM to small business owners. Focus on pain points like lost messages and unprofessional replies."

**Prompt for Benefits:**
> "Write 5 key benefits of using a WhatsApp CRM. Each benefit must be under 75 characters and start with a verb. Example: 'Organize every conversation.'"

**Prompt for Social Proof:**
> "Write 3 fake customer testimonials for a WhatsApp CRM. Each one should highlight a different benefit: one about saving time, one about increasing sales, and one about improving team collaboration." (Replace with real ones as you get them)

### Mobile-First
Samuel emphasizes this. Your page must look flawless and load instantly on mobile. Most traffic will be from mobile users.

## Step 3: The Facebook/Meta Ads Engine

This is the "slot machine" part. Your goal: find a winning ad creative + audience combination.

### Targeting Your Audience (The "Divorce Market")

**Layer 1 (Broad Interests):**
- Small Business Owners
- Entrepreneurs
- HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho, Pipedrive

**Layer 2 (Specific Interests):**
- WhatsApp Business
- Business Messenger
- Customer Support Software
- Sales Automation

**Layer 3 (Behaviors):**
- Business Page Administrators
- Engaged Shoppers

**The Gold Mine (Lookalikes):**
Once you have 100+ email sign-ups or customers, upload that list to Facebook and create a 1% Lookalike Audience. This will be your highest-performing audience.

### Creating Ad Creatives

**Static Images are Your Best Friend** (Don't waste time on expensive video. Use Canva.)

| Image Type | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Before & After** | Messy WhatsApp chat list (left) vs. clean 21staff inbox (right) |
| **Pain Point** | Text-based graphic: "Is This Your WhatsApp?" or "Your WhatsApp is Leaking Money" |
| **AI Avatar Trick** | HeyGen avatar holding phone with CRM interface (more authentic than stock) |
| **Feature Spotlight** | Clean screenshot of killer feature (e.g., automated assignment) with benefit overlay |

### Ad Copy & Headlines

- Use ChatGPT-generated headlines
- Focus on "Us vs. Them": "Why Your Current CRM Fails at WhatsApp"
- Lead with the problem: "Tired of asking 'Sorry, who is this again?'"

### Campaign Setup & Budget

- Use **CBO (Campaign Budget Optimization)** â€” lets Facebook's algorithm auto-allocate budget
- Starting budget: ~Target CAC (e.g., $50/day if you can afford $50 CAC)
- Structure: 3-4 ad sets Ã— 3-4 ad creatives each
- Let run for 3-4 days before optimizing

## Step 4: The Math â€” Tracking CAC vs. LTV

**Non-negotiable:** Track numbers to know if your "slot machine" is rigged in your favor.

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Conversion Rate** | Sign-ups / Visitors | 3-5% |
| **CAC** | Ad Spend / Sign-ups | < LTV/3 |
| **LTV** | Avg Monthly Revenue Ã— Avg Customer Lifespan | $500+ (example) |

**Golden Rule:** CAC must be significantly less than LTV. Healthy ratio is 1:3.

Example:
- Plan: $50/month
- Avg lifespan: 10 months
- **LTV = $500**
- If CAC = $50 â†’ **1:10 ratio** (incredibly profitable)

---

## Step 5: Iterate and Scale

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| CAC too high | Ad creative not resonating or offer weak. Kill losers, create new ones |
| Conversion rate too low | Landing page needs work. Test new headline, bonus, or benefit order |
| Found winner (4% conv, $40 CAC) | Scale slowly. Increase budget. Then try winning creative on LinkedIn/Google Ads |

## Problem Angles (Copy Ideas)

### Angle 1: Pelanggan Anda Kabur karena WhatsApp Lambat?

- Pelanggan menanyakan harga, tapi Anda balas besoknya. Sudah pasti mereka beli di tempat lain.
- Tim Anda lupa follow-up pelanggan yang "minat dulu". Kesempatan emas terbuang sia-sia.
- Tidak ada catatan pesan dari pelanggan lama. Mereka merasa tidak dihargai.

### Angle 2: Tim WhatsApp Anda Bekerja Sendiri-Sendiri?

- Pelanggan A chat ke sales, B chat ke admin, C chat ke owner. Tidak ada yang tahu riwayat lengkapnya.
- Staff resign, bawa semua data pelanggan di WhatsApp pribadinya. Data bisnis Anda hilang.
- Anda harus terus-menerus forward pesan. Waktu Anda habis untuk jadi kurir, bukan bos.

### Angle 3: Bosan Copy-Paste Pesan yang Sama Setiap Hari?

- Mengetik ulang alamat, ongkir, dan info produk yang sama ratusan kali.
- Mencari chat lama dengan pelanggan seperti mencari jarum dalam tumpukan jerami.
- Tidak ada sistem untuk ingatkan follow-up.

### Angle 4: 52% UMKM Digital Naik Omset. Ini Cara Anda Mulai.

**Based on data:** UMKM yang mengadopsi digital melaporkan peningkatan pendapatan.

- Langkah pertama: Integrasikan WhatsApp Anda ke dalam satu sistem yang terorganisir.
- Hasil langsung: Tangani lebih banyak pelanggan, tingkatkan follow-up, lihat omset tumbuh.

### Angle 5: Investasi Digital Pertama Anda yang Paling Menguntungkan

71% UMKM akan meningkatkan investasi digital mereka. Mulailah dengan yang paling berdampak langsung: **sistem WhatsApp Anda**.

- Jangan buang budget: Fokus pada alat yang Anda gunakan setiap hari.
- Investasi kecil, hasil besar
- Fondasi yang kuat untuk langkah digital selanjutnya

## Real Use Case / Testimonial Video Strategy

**Why this matters:** In Indonesian business culture, peer recommendation (rekomendasi) is one of the most powerful marketing tools. A real customer's journey builds unshakeable trust.

### Step 1: Find Your "Hero" Customer

You don't need a big production. Just one happy, early customer willing to share their story.

**Who to look for:**
- Enthusiastic UMKM owner
- Has seen clear, quick win with your CRM
- Mentioned it in support chat or gave positive feedback

**The Offer (Win-Win):**
> "Kami mau bikinkan video kesaksian singkat untuk Pak/Bu [Nama]. Sebagai terima kasih, kami berikan diskon 50% untuk 3 bulan ke depan atau gratis perpanjangan 1 bulan."

**Why this works:**
- Far cheaper than professional photoshoot
- Authentic story money can't buy

### Step 2: The "DIY" Video Shoot

**No pro crew needed.** A modern smartphone is enough.

**The Setup:**
- Good natural light (near window)
- Cheap tripod or prop phone against books
- Tidy background that represents their business

**The Interview (Keep it Simple):**

| Question | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| "Sebelum pakai 21staff, masalah terbesar apa yang Bapak/Ibu alami dengan WhatsApp?" | Hook â€” their pain |
| "Bagaimana 21staff membantu mengatasi masalah itu?" | Solution â€” how it works |
| "Apa hasil terbaik yang Anda dapatkan sejak menggunakannya?" | Result â€” the win |

**B-Roll Footage:**
After interview, film them using the product:
- Hands on phone
- Scrolling through dashboard
- Assigning task to team member

### Step 3: Create Authentic Ad Assets

**For Static Ads:**
- Take high-quality screenshot from video (customer smiling/talking)
- Use Canva to add powerful headline

Examples:
- "Omset Naik 30% dengan Sistem WhatsApp Ini."
- "Budi Santoso: 'Tim Saya Jadi Lebih Efisien.'"
- "Kisah Nyata: Dari Kacau ke Teratur di WhatsApp."

**For Video Ads (30-45 sec):**

| Segment | Duration | Content |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Hook | 3s | Start with problem: "Dulu, pesan pelanggan selalu ketinggalan..." |
| Solution | 15s | Show them using 21staff dashboard (B-roll) |
| Result | 10s | Best interview clip about positive outcome |
| CTA | 5s | Logo + "Coba 21staff.com Sekarang" |

**Pro Tip:** Always add captions! Many watch without sound.
