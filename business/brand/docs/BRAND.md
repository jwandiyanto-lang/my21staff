# my21staff Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Name:** my21staff
**Tagline:** No system, no growth.
**Website:** my21staff.com

---

## Logo

### Wordmark
The wordmark uses **Plus Jakarta Sans** at weight 800 (Extra Bold).

| Part | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| my | Dark Brown | `#2D2A26` |
| 21 | Orange | `#F7931A` |
| staff | Dark Brown | `#2D2A26` |

### Usage
- Always keep "my21staff" as one word, lowercase
- The "21" must always be orange
- Minimum clear space: height of the "2" on all sides
- Never stretch, rotate, or alter colors

### Logo Files
```
business/brand/logos/
├── wordmark-full.svg      # Primary logo
├── icon-only.svg          # Icon mark only
└── *.png                  # Various sizes (32, 64, 128, 256)
```

## Typography

### Primary Font
**Plus Jakarta Sans** — Used for headings, logo, emphasis

### Secondary Font
**Inter** — Used for body text, UI elements

### Font Weights
- Extra Bold (800): Logo, main headings
- Bold (700): Subheadings, buttons
- Semibold (600): Labels, emphasis
- Regular (400): Body text

## Voice & Tone

### Core Message
> **AI handles the routine. Humans handle the complex.**

### Personality
- **Direct:** No fluff, get to the point
- **Confident:** We know what works
- **Practical:** Focus on outcomes, not features
- **Human:** Warm but professional

### Do Say
- "Your 24/7 digital workforce"
- "Scale without the chaos"
- "The system that works while you sleep"
- "WhatsApp automation"
- "AI-powered chat"

### Don't Say
- ~~Kapso~~ (internal tech, never mention to customers)
- ~~Bot~~ (use "integration" or "AI staff" instead)
- ~~Kapso API~~ (use "messaging system" instead)
- ~~Chatbot~~ (use "AI assistant" or "digital staff")

## Imagery

### Style
- Bright, well-lit environments
- Modern retail/office spaces
- Real people (not stock photo poses)
- Clean, minimalist compositions

### Subjects
- Small business owners
- Retail stores, cafes, service businesses
- Mobile phones showing WhatsApp
- Team collaboration

### Avoid
- Dark, moody lighting
- Overly corporate imagery
- Outdated technology
- Cluttered compositions

---

## UI Components

### Buttons
- Primary: Dark Green (`#284b31`) with white text
- Secondary: Orange (`#F7931A`) with white text
- Outline: Border with text color matching

### Cards
- White background
- Subtle shadow (`shadow-lg` or `shadow-xl`)
- Rounded corners (`rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl`)

### Status Indicators
- Active/Online: Green (`#22c55e`)
- Warning: Orange (`#F7931A`)
- Error: Red (`#ef4444`)
