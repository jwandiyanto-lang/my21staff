## Section-by-Section Analysis

### 1. HERO SECTION

**Grade: B-**

**Working:**
- "No System, No Growth" headline nails the pain point
- CTA "Dapatkan Sistemnya" reinforces message
- StaffDeck card adds visual interest

**Issues:**

| Problem | Impact | Fix |
|---------|--------|-----|
| Inconsistent headline styling | Looks accidental — "No System," bold, "*No Growth.*" italic | Unify styling or make emphasis intentional |
| Generic subhead | "Software yang bertumbuh bareng bisnis Anda" is filler | Replace: "Semua leads WhatsApp di satu dashboard. Response dalam menit, bukan jam." |
| StaffDeck shows irrelevant data | "Sari - Laporan Keuangan" — not CRM related | Show Lead Card with name, status (Hot/Warm), source, last message |
| No trust indicators | Visitors don't know credibility | Add: "Dipercaya 50+ UMKM di Indonesia" |

---

### 2. PROBLEM SECTION — "Katanya bisnis itu enak..."

**Grade: B**

**Working:**
- Emotional hook with problem cards
- Real photos of frustrated business owners
- "Katanya" framing is relatable

**Issues:**

| Problem | Impact | Fix |
|---------|--------|-----|
| No transition to solution | Problems stated but no bridge to answer | Add: "Ini sebabnya mereka butuh sistem." |
| Missing AIDA bridge | Should state "my21staff IS the system" | Add transition text after problem cards |

---

### 3. SUPPORT SECTION — "Support 24/7 dari tim kami"

**Grade: C+**

**Working:**
- Human support promise is strong differentiator
- "Anda tanya, kami kerjakan, Anda lanjut" is excellent
- Checklist format clear

**Issues:**

| Problem | Impact | Fix |
|---------|--------|-----|
| Placeholder image | Yellow/orange gradient silhouette feels unfinished | Use real photo of support person |
| "GRATIS SELAMANYA" badge confusing | You have paid tiers — mixed signals | Clarify: "Support gratis di semua paket" |
| Generic chat bubble | "Halo! Ada yang bisa kami bantu?" | Show real scenario: "Pak, landing page-nya sudah live ya" |

---

### 4. PRICING SECTION

**Grade: B-**

**Working:**
- Two clear tiers: Solo (Rp3.9jt) and Team (Rp7.9jt)
- "Popular" badge on Team
- Feature bullets scannable
- Setup fee mentioned

**Issues:**

| Problem | Impact | Fix |
|---------|--------|-----|
| Missing UMR anchor | "Price of one employee" is killer comparison | Add: "Harga satu karyawan. Hasil satu tim." |
| Feature-focused, not benefit-focused | "200 pesan marketing/bulan" — so what? | Reframe: "200 pesan follow-up otomatis/bulan (cukup untuk 50 leads aktif)" |
| Vague target audience | "Untuk founder & freelancer" too generic | Make specific: "Punya <50 leads/bulan? Solo. Lebih dari itu? Team." |
| Setup fee hidden in footnote | Rp7.5jt significant — could feel like bait & switch | Feature it: "Termasuk setup website + WhatsApp + konsultasi bisnis" |
